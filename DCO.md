# Developer Certificate of Origin

Contributions to this repository must include a `Signed-off-by` line in each commit message.

By signing off a commit, the contributor certifies that they have the right to submit the contribution under this project's license and that the contribution may be redistributed as part of this project.

Use:

```text
Signed-off-by: Name <email@example.com>
```

Maintainers may ask contributors to amend commits that are missing sign-off evidence before merge.
